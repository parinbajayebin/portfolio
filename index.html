About Me

I'm a Computer Science student and Backend & AI Engineer passionate about building scalable software and intelligent AI-powered systems.

My journey began with web development using PHP and MySQL, where I built full-stack applications and learned the fundamentals of software engineering. As my interests evolved, I expanded into Python, FastAPI, machine learning, and Generative AI, focusing on systems that combine modern backend architecture with Large Language Models.

Today, I design and deploy applications that leverage Retrieval-Augmented Generation, vector search, and semantic retrieval to deliver accurate, context-aware responses from domain-specific knowledge bases.

I enjoy continuously learning new technologies and applying them through practical projects that solve real-world problems.
